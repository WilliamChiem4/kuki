# KUKI App

The KUKI app is not a menu — it's membership in a baking studio. A bakery app that's just a checkout funnel gets deleted between orders, because all its value lives at the moment of purchase. KUKI's single-cookie focus is the unlock: the app is where you find out what we're baking next, watch your batch go from oven to door, and hold a balance worth coming back for. The retention engine is **scarcity, ritual, and collection** — closer to a sneaker-drop app or a record club than to a delivery app.

The full argument — purpose, personas, engagement loops, business model, MVP, roadmap, and success metrics — lives in **[docs/PRODUCT_STRATEGY.md](docs/PRODUCT_STRATEGY.md)**. Read that first.
